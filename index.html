Visitor Guide

The Best of Carolina Beach

Real picks. Local knowledge. No fluff — and zero government spin.

Boardwalk Legend
Britt's Donut Shop
Since 1939 · Cash only · Worth every minute

Hot glazed donuts made fresh and open right in front of you on the boardwalk every summer night. Lines wrap the corner. If you do one thing on the boardwalk, it's this.

BoardwalkCash OnlyIconic
Seafood
Capt'n Bill's Backyard Grill
Local catch · Casual · Decades of loyalty

Fresh-caught local seafood in a no-pretense backyard setting. The shrimp basket and hush puppies are legendary. Regulars have been coming back for decades.

SeafoodCasualLocal
Waterfront Dining
The Deck House
Fish tacos · Ocean views · Sunset dining

Grab a deck table and let the ocean breeze do its thing. Fresh local catch in the tacos and a sunset view that ruins you for everywhere else.

WaterfrontTacosSunset
Coffee & Breakfast
Beach Brew Co.
Strong espresso · Açaí bowls · Patio life

The best way to start a beach day. Strong espresso, açaí bowls, breakfast sandwiches. The patio fills fast — arrive early and claim the corner table.

CoffeeBreakfastPatio
Dessert
Island Creamery
Handcrafted · Seasonal · Family tradition

Handcrafted small-batch ice cream with rotating seasonal flavors. The salted caramel is legendary. Post-beach ice cream here becomes an annual family ritual fast.

Ice CreamFamilySeasonal
Dive Bar
Ol' Luther's
Locals only energy · Cold beer · No pretense

Walk in a stranger, leave knowing half the room. Cold beer, cold AC, conversations till closing. The quintessential Carolina Beach local bar.

LocalDiveCash Friendly
Live Music Bar
Shuckin' Shack Oyster Bar
Oysters · Live music · Summer nights

Cold oysters on the half shell, cold drinks, live music on weekends. One of the best summer-night vibes on the island. Get there early on Fridays.

Live MusicOystersWeekends
Beach Bar
Pelican Pete's Cantina
Frozen drinks · Waterfront · Sandy feet welcome

Frozen margaritas, waterfront seats, sunset that ruins you for everywhere else. Sandy feet welcome — this place wouldn't have it any other way.

WaterfrontFrozen DrinksCasual
Gifts & Souvenirs
Shell Shop & Co.
Local shells · Handmade jewelry · Community institution

Best selection of local shells and handmade coastal gifts on the island. Staff know their regulars by name. Represents the town's soul better than any other shop.

Surf & Outdoor
Liquid Logic
Surf gear · Kayaks · Real earned advice

Boards, kayaks, and a staff that actually surfs and paddles. The advice here is earned, not Googled. Come here before you get in the water for anything.

Not the generic tourist tee. Artist-designed pieces rooted in actual Carolina Beach culture. The kind of shirt that starts real conversations back home.

A proper independent bookshop with a strong local section. Coastal history, local authors, the beach reads you actually want. Community bulletin board inside.

Best for Families
Vacation Rental Houses
VRBO · Airbnb · Book in January

For families, renting a house is the only move. Kitchen, yard, parking, space to breathe. The good ones vanish in January for summer. Set a reminder and move fast.

Hotel
Carolina Beach Hotel
Beachfront · Pool · Boardwalk steps away

Right on the ocean with direct beach access and a pool. Walking distance to the boardwalk. Great if you want hotel amenities without leaving the island.

BeachfrontPoolBoardwalk
Camping
Carolina Beach State Park
Full hookups · Marsh views · Reserve early

Camp inside the state park. Full hookups available. Waking up to marsh sounds and birdsong before heading to the beach is one of those quiet magic things.

NatureBudgetReserve Early
Iconic Experience
Freeman Park
Drive on the beach · 4WD required · Permit required

Drive your 4WD vehicle right onto the beach at the northern end of the island. Fire pits at night, pelicans by day. Let tires down to ~20 PSI before hitting sand.

4WDPermitMust Do
Nature
Carolina Beach State Park
Venus flytraps · Hiking · Marsh trails

One of the only places on Earth to see wild Venus flytraps. Miles of trails, a marina, and some of the most beautiful natural scenery on the Cape Fear coast.

HikingNatureHidden Gem
Aquarium
Fort Fisher Aquarium
Sharks · Sea turtles · Half-day minimum

One of NC's best aquariums. Sharks, loggerhead sea turtles, native fish. A half-day easily disappears here, especially with kids who have any curiosity at all.

FamilyEducationFort Fisher
Wildlife
Dolphin Spotting on the ICW
Free · Dawn & dusk · Intracoastal Waterway

Head to the ICW at dawn or dusk. Bottlenose dolphins feed in the channel daily. The bridge toward Wilmington is a great free viewing spot most people miss.

WildlifeFreeICW
Freeman Park
4WD on the Beach
Real 4WD only · ~20 PSI · Drive at low tide

AWD won't cut it — you need actual 4WD. Let tires down to ~20 PSI before hitting sand. Drive at low tide. Always bring a pump to reinflate before leaving.

Freeman ParkPro Tip
Timing
Best Sunrise Spot
North end · Before 6am · Zero crowds

Walk or drive to the far north end near Freeman Park before 6am. Wide open sand, not a soul around, and a Carolina sunrise that makes you understand why people never leave.

SunrisePhotographyNorth End
Timing
Beat the Summer Crowds
Late May · Early September · Sweet spot

Late May and early September: warm water, fewer people, lower prices. The shoulder season here is genuinely special — the town feels like it belongs to whoever shows up.

Shoulder SeasonBest Value
Wildlife
Ghost Crab Hunting
After dark · Flashlight · Kids love it

After dark, grab a flashlight and head to the waterline. Ghost crabs scatter when you hit them with light. Fast, translucent, wild. A CB night tradition for every family.

For Locals & Regulars

The Community

This isn't just a vacation spot. It's a town with a pulse, a culture, and people who keep its soul alive year after year.

🎵
Live Music Scene

CB has a real live music culture — beach music, shag dancing, local bands on weeknights. The Shuckin' Shack and boardwalk bars keep it alive all summer long.

🎣
The Fishing Community

Surf fishermen, offshore captains, and pier regulars who carry irreplaceable knowledge of these waters. One of the most tight-knit communities on the coast.

🐢
Sea Turtle Watch

Local volunteers monitor nesting sea turtles on CB beaches every summer. One of the most beautiful quiet acts of community stewardship on the whole island.

🏄
Surf & Water Culture

A real surf community built over generations. Dawn patrol sessions, ICW paddleboard races, and a deep connection to the water that shapes everyone who lives here.

🎨
Local Artists

Painters, photographers, and craftspeople who draw constant inspiration from the light and landscape here. The boardwalk and local shops are full of their work.

🍽
The Restaurant Community

The restaurant and bar owners here know and support each other. That cooperative spirit makes this a place where small independent businesses genuinely thrive.

Why This Site Exists

We Keep Coming Back.
Every Single Year.

Carolina Beach isn't just a place on a map. It's the smell of sunscreen and salt air in the morning. It's Britt's donuts eaten every single day for breakfast. It's kids chasing ghost crabs at midnight and dolphins surfacing in the ICW at sunrise.

This site was built out of pure love for this town — its people, its businesses, its boardwalk culture, and the fiercely loyal community that keeps its soul alive year after year.

Whether you're a first-timer or a decades-long regular: you belong here.
